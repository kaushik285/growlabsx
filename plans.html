⚠️ Critical Safety Information
Medical Supervision Required: Any hormone-related interventions (HGH, aromatase inhibitors, peptides) MUST be supervised by a qualified endocrinologist. Self-administration can cause serious health complications including organ damage, hormonal imbalances, and permanent growth plate fusion.

Supplement Safety: Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting new supplements, especially if you have pre-existing conditions or take medications. Some supplements can interact with medications or cause adverse effects.

Realistic Expectations: Height is 80% genetic. These protocols optimize your genetic potential but cannot override your genetic blueprint. Focus on maximizing YOUR potential, not comparing to others.
